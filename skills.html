SKILLS

HOMEPAGE

Leadership

  • Leadership is about collaboration and inspiring other to do the best work. I am to be direct and collaborate with my team members by delegating tasks, leading by example, and making sure they know i care.

CREATIVITY

  • Creativity to me, is the ability to think outside the box and come up with original ideas. Be open to just playing , experimenting, enjoying, messing up and trying again. Creativity works best if you do it for the love of it rather than to be perfect.

GOAL SETTING

  • Goal setting lets you focus on the next step and helps you move in the direction you want. The body follows the mind so you need to stay focused and not lose motivation. Be always aware of why you should take specific actions and where they will lead you. Goals allow youn to measure progress

COMMUNICATION

  • Communication skills are the abilities you use when giving and receiving different kinds of information. Some examples include communicating new ideas, feelings or even an update on your project. Communication skills involve listening, speaking, observing and empathising.

CONFIDENCE

  • Self-confidence is an attitude about your skills and abilities. It means you accept and trust yourself and have a sense of control in your life. You know your strengths and weakness well, and have a positive view of yourself. You set realistic expectations and goals, communicate assertively and can handle criticism.
